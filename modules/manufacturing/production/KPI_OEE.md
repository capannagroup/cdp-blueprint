# Manufacturing KPIs & OEE

## Overview

Manufacturing KPIs measure the efficiency, quality, productivity, and utilization of manufacturing resources.

The primary KPI framework is Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE), supported by production, quality, maintenance, and labor metrics.

---

# Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)

OEE = Availability × Performance × Quality

---

## Availability

Measures actual running time versus planned production time.

Formula:

Availability =
Operating Time / Planned Production Time

Example:

480 min planned

450 min operating

Availability = 93.75%

---

## Performance

Measures machine speed compared to ideal cycle time.

Formula:

Performance =
Ideal Cycle Time × Total Parts
--------------------------------
Operating Time

---

## Quality

Measures first-pass good production.

Formula:

Quality =
Good Parts
-----------
Total Parts

---

## OEE Classification

| OEE | Status |
|------|--------|
| >85% | World Class |
| 75-85% | Excellent |
| 60-75% | Acceptable |
| <60% | Needs Improvement |
