# Shift Management

## Overview

Shift Management controls factory working shifts, manpower allocation, attendance, overtime, and production calendars. It ensures that operators, machines, and work centers are synchronized with production schedules.

---

## Objectives

- Manage production shifts
- Assign operators
- Control overtime
- Balance workloads
- Improve labor utilization
- Ensure production continuity

---

## Shift Types

- Morning Shift
- Evening Shift
- Night Shift
- Weekend Shift
- Overtime Shift

---

## Shift Calendar

The calendar defines:

- Working Days
- Holidays
- Factory Shutdowns
- Maintenance Days
- Overtime Days

---

## Shift Assignment

Each shift includes:

- Shift Supervisor
- Operators
- Work Centers
- Machines
- Planned Production
- Working Hours

---

## Attendance

Attendance records include:

- Check-In
- Check-Out
- Break Time
- Overtime
- Absence
- Leave

---

## Labor Planning

The system allocates:

- Operators
- Technicians
- Maintenance Staff
- Supervisors
- Helpers

---

## KPIs

- Attendance Rate
- Labor Utilization
- Overtime Hours
- Productivity per Shift
- Absenteeism Rate

---

## Integrations

- Operators
- Work Centers
- Machines
- Scheduling
- Production Orders
- HR
