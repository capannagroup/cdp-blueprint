# Andon System

## Overview

The Andon System provides real-time visual management for production activities. It immediately alerts operators, supervisors, maintenance, quality, and management whenever production abnormalities occur.

The objective is to minimize downtime, improve response time, and increase Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE).

---

# Objectives

- Visual production monitoring
- Immediate problem notification
- Faster response time
- Improve production flow
- Reduce downtime
- Increase productivity
- Support Lean Manufacturing

# Andon Colors

## Green

Machine running normally.

---

## Yellow

Attention required.

Examples:

- Material shortage
- Tool wear
- Waiting inspection

---

## Orange

Production issue.

Examples:

- Schedule delay
- Operator assistance
- Setup taking too long

---

## Red

Critical stop.

Examples:

- Machine failure
- Emergency stop
- Quality rejection

---

## Blue

Maintenance request.

---

## White

Information.
