# Operators

## Overview

The Operators component manages all production personnel involved in manufacturing activities. It maintains operator profiles, skills, certifications, work assignments, productivity metrics, and labor history.

Operators may be assigned to one or multiple Work Centers depending on qualifications and certifications.

---

## Objectives

- Manage operator information
- Track production assignments
- Validate operator certifications
- Measure labor productivity
- Monitor attendance
- Improve workforce utilization

---

## Operator Profile

Each Operator includes:

- Operator ID
- Employee Number
- Full Name
- Department
- Position
- Skill Level
- Certifications
- Assigned Work Centers
- Shift
- Employment Status

---

## Skill Levels

- Trainee
- Junior
- Intermediate
- Senior
- Specialist
- Supervisor

---

## Certifications
