# Production Planning

## Overview

Production Planning determines what products should be manufactured, when they should be produced, and which resources are required. It converts customer demand into executable production schedules while optimizing machine utilization, labor, and material availability.

---

## Objectives

- Plan production efficiently
- Balance machine capacity
- Optimize labor allocation
- Reduce bottlenecks
- Meet customer delivery dates
- Improve manufacturing efficiency

---

## Planning Levels

### Strategic Planning

- Monthly Capacity
- Quarterly Capacity
- Annual Production Targets

### Master Production Schedule (MPS)

- Weekly Production
- Customer Orders
- Forecast Orders
- Delivery Commitments

### Detailed Scheduling

- Daily Schedule
- Machine Assignment
- Operator Assignment
- Shift Planning

---

## Inputs

Production Planning uses:

- Customer Orders

- Available Inventory
- Production Orders
- BOM
- Routings
- Machine Capacity
- Labor Availability
- Material Availability

---

## Outputs

Planning generates:

- Production Schedule
- Machine Schedule
- Operator Schedule
- Material Requirements
- Work Orders
- Capacity Plan

---

## Capacity Planning

The system evaluates:

- Machine Capacity
- Labor Capacity
- Shift Capacity
- Overtime
- Available Hours
- Bottlenecks

## Scheduling Rules

- Rush Orders
