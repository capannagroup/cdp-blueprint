# Compliance

## Purpose

The Compliance framework ensures that the Capanna Digital Platform follows international security, privacy, and governance standards.

---

## Standards

### ISO 27001

Information Security Management System (ISMS)

Controls:

- Access Control
- Asset Management
- Incident Management
- Business Continuity
- Risk Management

---

### OWASP Top 10

Protection against:

- Broken Access Control
- Cryptographic Failures
- Injection
- Insecure Design
- Security Misconfiguration
- Vulnerable Components
- Authentication Failures
- Software Integrity Failures
- Logging Failures
- SSRF

---

### GDPR

Personal Data Protection

Requirements:

- User Consent
- Right to Access
- Right to Delete
- Data Portability
- Privacy by Design

---

### SOC 2

Security

Availability

Confidentiality

Processing Integrity

Privacy

---

### PCI DSS

Required for payment processing.

---

## Compliance Reviews

Compliance is reviewed:

- Quarterly
- Before Production Releases
- After Security Incidents

---

## Documentation

Compliance evidence includes:

- Audit Logs
- Security Reports
- Penetration Tests
- Vulnerability Assessments
- Risk Assessments
